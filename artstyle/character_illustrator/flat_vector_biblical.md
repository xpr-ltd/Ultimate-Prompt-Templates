Minimalist flat vector biblical character illustration style. Characters are designed with simplified geometric anatomy, smooth rounded shapes, and clean vector construction. Use a modern editorial flat-design aesthetic with soft muted pastel colors, minimal texture, and very low rendering detail. Characters should feel symbolic and modular rather than highly animated or emotionally exaggerated.

Body proportions should be slightly shortened compared to standard editorial vector figures — with a subtle chibi influence in overall height and proportions — but without becoming fully kawaii or super-deformed. Characters should appear slightly shorter and more compact than realistic adults, with moderately larger heads relative to the body, shortened legs, compact torsos, and simplified limbs while still preserving balanced human anatomy and recognizable biblical silhouettes. The chibi influence should remain restrained and tasteful, avoiding toddler-like proportions, oversized anime heads, or exaggerated cute aesthetics.

Faces should be minimal, calm, and readable with subtle emotional clarity:
* tiny or simplified eyes
* subtle mouths
* minimal eyebrows
* soft neutral expressions
* gentle smiles or mild concern
* emotional range (depending on character or scene description)
* simplified noses or implied facial structure
* no exaggerated cheek blush
* no sparkling anime eyes
* no highly expressive cartoon reactions
* no screaming, crying, or exaggerated comedic faces

Physical posing and gesticulation should remain soft, symbolic, and readable:
* gentle hand gestures
* calm open-arm poses
* subtle pointing or blessing gestures
* simplified walking or standing poses
* mild storytelling gestures
* restrained body language
* relaxed posture
* iconic silhouette readability
* no exaggerated action poses
* no dynamic superhero anatomy
* no slapstick cartoon movement
* no extreme facial or body distortion

Use smooth vector fills with:
* no heavy outlines
* no sketch strokes
* no painterly texture
* no watercolor effects
* no photorealistic rendering
* no 3D shading
* no glossy highlights
* no cel shading
* no airbrush gradients

Color palette:
* muted earth tones
* dusty blues
* soft browns
* pale creams
* desaturated reds and greens
* calm educational tones
* avoid neon saturation or hyper-vibrant palettes

Character construction:
* soft geometric forms
* tapered limbs
* rounded hands
* simplified robes and garments
* minimal folds
* flat layered shapes
* clean silhouette readability
* isolated character poses
* lots of whitespace
* sticker-pack or clipart-pack composition

The style should resemble:
* modern stock vector illustration
* educational religious clipart
* editorial infographic characters
* explainer-animation vector assets
* SVG flat character packs

NOT the following styles:
* NOT kawaii anime
* NOT Disney-style cartoon
* NOT Pixar-style 3D
* NOT preschool mascot illustration
* NOT rubber hose cartoon
* NOT comic-book art
* NOT hand-drawn sketch art
* NOT watercolor illustration
* NOT realistic anatomy
* NOT detailed fantasy painting
* NOT highly expressive children’s cartoon style
* NOT thick-outline sticker art
* NOT glossy corporate vector style
* NOT meme/cartoon caricature style

Biblical elements should remain simple and iconic:
* staffs
* robes
* ark
* stone tablets
* halos
* slings
* scrolls
* sheep
* simple symbolic props

Overall feeling:
clean, calm, educational, timeless, modular, friendly, minimal, modern, symbolic, softly expressive, gently chibi-influenced, and suitable for Bible charades, learning games, flashcards, educational apps, and animated explainer systems.
